Resolution and it's impact

I believe that Resolution has had a HUGE impact on our society and where the future is going. It seems that every time we turn around, a new TV or monitor is being released. Are you into gaming? There are TVs/Monitors for that! Sports? Yep, there's a screen for that. Movies! You bet. Anything that you like to do on a screen, there is something that is the BEST for you. The latest, is 4k which provides an amazing experience for no matter what you're doing. It's the most crisp, clear, vibrant screen yet. There are so many options, including OLED and QLED screens. It really comes down to what is the best option for you and your viewing/gaming/working experience. Before you know it 8k will be the new standard. Who knows where we go from here! The posibilities are endless.
